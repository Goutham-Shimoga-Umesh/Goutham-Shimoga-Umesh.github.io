Pentagon Building — Crosswind Loading (3D)

3D Bluff Body k–ε (steady)

Steady RANS (k–ε) simulation of crosswind over a pentagon‑plan building in a virtual wind tunnel. Inlet profile: V = 0.8·z (m/s) from z=0 to 15 m. Two orientations were tested: (Run 1) wind normal to a flat face, (Run 2) wind toward a vertex.

  • Domain: Cylindrical wind tunnel (L=200 cm, R=60 cm)
  • Fluid: Air, ρ=const, μ=const (Fluent DB)
  • BCs: Velocity inlet (profile), pressure outlet, side/top walls = slip, ground + building = no‑slip
  • Outputs: y‑velocity & static pressure contours at z=1.25 m; total drag & split (pressure vs viscous)

Total drag and split by source were recorded using report definitions on the building walls.

  • Run 1: total drag is dominated by the pressure term (form drag).
