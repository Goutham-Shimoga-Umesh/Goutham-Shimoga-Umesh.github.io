Pentagon Building — Crosswind Loading (3D)

External Aerodynamics · 3D bluff body · ANSYS Fluent

Steady RANS (k–ω SST) Y-plane symmetry Force decomposition: pressure vs viscous

Overview

Objective was to quantify side-loading on a pentagon-section building in cross-flow and separate drag into pressure and viscous components. Two runs were performed with different inlet conditions (Run 1 and Run 2). Post-processing was carried out on the symmetry plane for static pressure and transverse (y) velocity to visualize wake development and recirculation.

  • Solver: steady RANS, k–ω SST; second-order upwind.
  • Domain: rectangular wind tunnel with geometric symmetry about mid-y plane.
  • BCs: inlet velocity profile, pressure outlet, symmetry, no-slip walls.
  • Outputs: total drag, pressure drag, viscous drag; pressure & y-velocity contours.
